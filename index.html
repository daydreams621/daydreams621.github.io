I teach art, and I enjoy doing illustrations in my daily life.

I am an art teacher. I teach adult drawing including sketch, watercolor, oilpainting and also involved in children art. In my daily life I do my own drawing and likes to find inpiration from things around me. Drawing is all about presently myself and my thoughts through things in daily life. It is important for me to learn things every of my drawings, too. In the drawing process, I learn to look back in myself to present how I feel and being honest through every moment of my artwork. Through this process I hope to be able to help people in a positive and encouraging way. Bringing warm and comfort is what I like. This is what I aim for.
